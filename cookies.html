Cookie Policy.

What cookies this website uses, why, and how you can control them.

Last updated: 5 July 2026

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files that a website stores on your device. They let the site remember things about your visit, such as your language or your cookie choices, and they can be used to measure how the site is used.

2. Our approach

When you first visit, we show you a cookie banner. We do not load any analytics or non-essential cookies until you choose “Accept”. If you decline, only the strictly necessary items below are used.

3. Cookies we use

Strictly necessary (always active)

  • Your cookie choice — we remember whether you accepted or declined, stored locally in your browser, so we do not ask you again on every page.

Analytics (only with your consent)

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — helps us understand, in aggregate, which pages are useful and how visitors find us. Sets cookies such as _ga and _ga_<id>. These are only set after you accept. You can withdraw your consent at any time.

4. Other services

The website loads fonts from Google Fonts and is hosted by Netlify. These providers do not set advertising cookies through this site, but they may process technical data such as your IP address to deliver content and keep the site secure. See our Privacy Policy for more.

5. How to change your choice

You can change or withdraw your cookie choice at any time by clicking “Cookie settings” at the bottom of any page. You can also block or delete cookies through your browser settings; if you do, some parts of websites may not work as expected.

6. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy. The current version is always the one on this page, with its last-updated date shown above.
